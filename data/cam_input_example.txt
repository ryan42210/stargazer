# instruction
The input from camera should follow format above
fov_x, fov_y should be in degrees.
resolution_x, resolution_y should be positive integers.
star_point_x and star_point_y \in [0, resolution_x(y)] in float
mag be floating point numbers, indicating magnitude of stars.
